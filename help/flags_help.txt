**How can I change my flag on tables?**
To change your flag on future tables, use the command `{SERVER_PREFIX}setflag flagcode`
To remove your flag from future tables, use the command `{SERVER_PREFIX}setflag`
*Example:* `{SERVER_PREFIX}setflag ca` sets your flag to the Canadian flag.
*Example:* `{SERVER_PREFIX}setflag` removes your flag from future tables.

**What flag do I currently have set?**
MKW Table Bot will send a picture of your flag if you use the command `{SERVER_PREFIX}getflag`

Flags and their corresponding flag codes can be found at <https://gb.hlorenzi.com/help/flags>
